01 — The Gap那个缺口

Your automation stack has a hole in it 你的自动化流水线，在支付这儿断了

Agents handle everything from sourcing to fulfillment. But the payment layer — FX conversion, supplier payments, compliance — is still manual. No API. No webhook. No sandbox. We want to fix that, and we need your help to understand exactly what's missing. 从选品到发货，Agents 跑得很顺。但一涉及到钱——换汇、跨境打款、合规审查——系统就哑火了。没 API 能接，没 Webhook 能监，连 Sandbox 都不给试。我们想拆掉它，但得先听听：你卡在哪儿了？
