The problem

Your agent can sell on 50 platforms overnight.
But can it collect from all of them?

📦

Fragmented Payouts

Every marketplace pays differently. Amazon settles biweekly in local currency. Shopify pays daily. TikTok Shop varies by region. Your agent doesn't care — but your bank does.

💱

Currency Chaos

A single AI store selling across 10 markets generates payouts in 10 currencies. Without automated FX and consolidation, you're losing margin on every conversion.

🛡

Compliance at Scale

An agent that opens 20 new marketplace stores this quarter doesn't know about KYC requirements, payout verification, or regulatory thresholds. WorldFirst handles all of it.
